Rundburg

The central city and most important hub in the world.

Rundburg is the central city of Wandel. From here, players can reach the market, guild, crafting stations and routes into several regions.

Key Features

  • Guild Board for Quests
  • Market and Merchants
  • Forge and Smelter
  • Player-to-player trading
  • Waypoints to Surrounding Areas

Expansion

Additional interiors such as a tavern, farm, alchemy area and more NPC locations can make the city denser and more alive later on.

Rundburg’s final lore, political structure and history remain open and can be expanded later.
