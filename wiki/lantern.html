Lantern

An equippable light source for caves and nighttime.

The lantern improves visibility and atmosphere in dark areas. It has its own idle and running animations and hangs correctly in the character’s hand.

Technical Properties

  • No Collision
  • No Shadows from Invisible Helper Parts
  • Sufficient Brightness for Caves and Nighttime
  • Compatible with Movement and Other Systems

Gameplay Role

The lantern makes darkness a deliberate part of exploration without leaving players unable to see.
