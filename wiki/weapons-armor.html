Weapons & Armor

Equipment, material tiers, drops and sets.

Equipment is a central part of progression and is obtained through crafting, enemy drops and quests.

Weapons

The game is designed around multiple weapon classes. Enemies have a chance to drop the weapon they carry.

Drop Tiers

MaterialExample Drop Chance
Rusty50 %
Iron40 %
Steel30 %
Mithril20 %

Armor Sets

  • Rundburg Guard Armor – Iron
  • Heavy Knight Armor – Steel
  • Magic Knight Armor – Mithril

Armor affects stats and has a separate inventory section. Hiding the helmet only changes its appearance.
