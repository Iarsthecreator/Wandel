Alchemy

Gather herbs, create powders and brew potions.

Alchemy is a separate production path alongside forging and smelting.

Processing

  1. Find and gather plants in the world.
  2. Process the herbs into powder at a station.
  3. Combine multiple ingredients into a potion recipe.
  4. Use the finished potion from the inventory or hotbar.

Stations

Herb-to-powder processing and potion brewing have dedicated locations, including an area near the magic tower.

Quality

Potions have models, liquid colors, drinking animations, sounds and matching light or VFX effects.
