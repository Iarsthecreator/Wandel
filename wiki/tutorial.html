Tutorial

A guided introduction to the world of Wandel.

The tutorial is designed so players learn the most important systems directly through gameplay.

Sequence

  1. Pick up the dagger and learn the first combat movements.
  2. Receive an axe and fight your first goblin.
  3. Gather clover and sell it to the merchant.
  4. Process rusty material at the smelter.
  5. Craft an iron dagger at the forge.
  6. Receive a pickaxe and mine coal and iron.
  7. Leave the tutorial forest and find the road to Rundburg.
  8. Learn about important locations such as the market, trading area and guild board.
Markers and steps will continue to be polished during development so new players can progress without additional explanations.

Goal

After the tutorial, every player should understand the basics of combat, gathering, crafting, trading and quests.
