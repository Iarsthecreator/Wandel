Quests & Guild Board

Missions, quest offers, guild points and rewards.

The guild board offers several quest options at the same time. Players can choose suitable missions and later receive rewards.

Refresh

After a timer, the board fully refreshes with new options. An additional refresh can be triggered with gems. Both methods are intended to restore the full number of offers.

Quest Types

  • Defeat Enemies
  • Gather Resources
  • Reach Locations
  • Craft or Turn In Items
  • Night and Regional Quests

Guild Progression

Quests can grant guild points. In the long term, rank benefits, rewards, unlocks and additional systems are possible.
