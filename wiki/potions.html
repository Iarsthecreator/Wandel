Potions

Health, mana and regeneration in small and large variants.

Potions are consumable items with their own models, liquid colors, animations and sounds.

TypeEffectVisual Theme
HealthRestores a percentage of health.Red
ManaRestores a percentage of mana.Blue
RegenerationGrants additional regeneration over time.Green, Pulsing

Sizes

Small variants use narrow bottles and a green rarity presentation. Large variants use wider bottles and a blue presentation.
