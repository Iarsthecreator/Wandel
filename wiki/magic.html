Magic

Mana, spells, casting and the planned expansion of the magic system.

Magic is a central part of the upcoming progression. The existing fire abilities are being polished and will be expanded later.

Mana

Mana scales with the corresponding stat. Spent energy regenerates over time and is restored after death.

Casting

Stronger spells can lock the player in place while casting. Animations, hand effects, lock-on and a dedicated crosshair clearly communicate the state.

Planned Expansion

  • Additional Spells and Magic Types
  • Wizard NPC and Magic Quests
  • Magic Tower as an Important Location
  • Magical Forest Accessible by Teleporter
  • Possible Status Effects Such as Burning, Ignition or Smoke
