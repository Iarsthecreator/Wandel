Crafting, Forging & Smelting

Process resources and craft new items.

Crafting connects gathering with progression. Ores and other raw materials are processed through several stations before becoming finished equipment.

Smelting

Coal can be used as fuel. Iron and mithril are processed into bars. Successful actions have their own sounds and feedback.

Forging

Bars and other materials are turned into weapons or equipment at the forge. Single and bulk actions may have different costs and sounds.
