Inventory & Hotbar

Manage, search, filter and equip items, and assign hotbar slots.

The inventory displays items in a grid with search, filtering and rarity presentation. A separate hotbar provides quick access.

Hotbar Assignment

When an item is selected, a hotbar slot can be clicked directly to assign that item.

Quality Improvements

  • Larger Item Icons
  • Clear Selection Highlight on the Outer Border
  • Smooth Hover Enlargement
  • Animated Opening and Closing of the Information Panel
  • Search and Sorting Without Disruptive Slot Movement

Equipment

Weapons, tools and armor use dedicated slots. The helmet can be shown or hidden without affecting its stats.
