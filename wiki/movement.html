Movement

Sprinting, rolling, dashing, double jumping and shift-lock.

Movement is an essential part of the combat feel. Wandel uses quick directional changes and evasive actions without making attacks completely unrestricted.

Core Actions

  • Standard Movement and Sprinting
  • Forward Dash
  • Rolling with a Short Dodge Window
  • Double Jump
  • Shift-Lock with a Dedicated Crosshair

Movement While Casting

Movement is heavily restricted during stronger spells so casting animations and risk remain clear. Fast basic spells may be exempt from this rule.

Equipment Compatibility

Tools such as the pickaxe, axe or lantern should not permanently block movement abilities. This interaction continues to be tested with new items.
