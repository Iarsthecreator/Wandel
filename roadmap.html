Roadmap

A clear overview of completed, active and planned areas.

Many core systems are now stable enough for new gameplay content and map areas to be added more quickly.

Inventory, stats, party, trading, quests, crafting, gathering and central UI structures.

Herbs, powders, small and large potions, as well as models, VFX, sounds and animations.

World, UI and Presentation

More map detail, new locations, final UI polish and video recording.
