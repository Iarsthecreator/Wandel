Getting Started

The tutorial guides new players step by step through combat, gathering, selling, crafting and the most important locations in the world.

The exact sequence may still change during development. The website therefore distinguishes between implemented and planned content.

Controls

ActionDescription
MoveStandard movement using a keyboard or controller.
AttackBasic attacks and weapon-specific combat actions.
DodgingRoll or dash with a short dodge window.
MenuOpens the inventory, stats, party, shop and settings.
Toggle HelmetThe helmet can be shown or hidden using its assigned key.

The currently valid keybinds are shown directly in the game settings menu.

Progression

Enemies, quests and other activities grant experience. Leveling up awards stat points for vitality, strength, intelligence and mana. Guild points provide an additional progression path.

Typical Gameplay Loop

  1. Choose a quest from the guild board.
  2. Explore a region and find enemies or resources.
  3. Collect rewards, plants, ores or equipment.
  4. Sell, smelt or forge items, or process them into potions.
  5. Improve your stats and enter more difficult areas.

Important Notes

  • Wandel is in development; balance and content may change.
  • The official Roblox game, Roblox group and Discord links are available throughout the website.
  • The wiki displays both existing and planned content with status labels.
