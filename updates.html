Development Log

News & Updates

The most important changes from Wandel’s ongoing development.

July 2026UI & Quality

Inventory and Settings Reworked

The inventory interface received larger item displays, clearer selection highlights, smoother animations and a notice explaining that hotbar slots can be clicked directly. The settings menu now also displays controls and keybinds.

July 2026Alchemy

Potions Fully Integrated into the Game

Health, mana and regeneration potions now have models, animations, drinking sounds and matching visual effects. Herbs can be processed into powders and then used in recipes.

July 2026Magic

Magic System Polished

New effects, improved movement rules while casting, a dedicated shift-lock crosshair state and revised casting animations increase the quality of the abilities.

New Beginner and Night Enemies

A slime enemy is planned for early quests. Zombie enemies and matching missions are planned for the graveyard at night.
